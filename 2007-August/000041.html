Hi Stefan,

on my System "du -sch *.a" gives

1.4M    libPackets.a
3.8M    libPackets_DefaultBundle.a
1.4M    libPackets_MPEGDVBBundle.a
6.4M    libPPI.a
949K    libScheduler.a
5.2M    libSocket.a
100K    libUtils.a
19M     total

So we are talking about 19M, not 114M, unless we want the pain of also building separate doc-packages.

And I can not really think about a real-world application, that uses just one of the libraries. Similar to boost, most users will start installing one package and end up with... well: more than one :). And: the fokus of senf is much narrower than that of boost, anyway.

Conclusion: we (Jens and Achim) stongly prefer *one* debian package.

Greetings
Achim
