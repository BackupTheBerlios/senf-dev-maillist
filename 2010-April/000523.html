Summary: scheduler with TimerEvents only

Details: This problem has only be observed by only use TimerEvents in the Scheduler::proccess(). Hence it might occur also in other circumstances.

If you have two TimerEvents t1 and t2 and both are enabled only the first timeout will call its callback.

This could be reproduced with the Unittest senf/Scheduler/TimerEventProxy.test.cc by commenting out the 10000msec timer
