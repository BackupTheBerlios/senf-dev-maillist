Hi everyone,

I have implemented a workaround which (hopefully) fixes the race
concerning Boost.DateTime and logging/scheduler by temporarily
removing formated date output from the logger.

This fix introduces the usage of POSIX clocks and on some
distributions, this mandates linking against librt. So

  EVERYONE ADD -lrt TO THEIR LINK COMMAND

Thank you. And whoever asks again whye 'clock_gettime' is not defined
when linking will be officially executed :-)

stefan.
