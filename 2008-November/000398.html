Since I became increasingly annoyed by the immense SENF link times I
have revisted that area.

I found, that when linking with 'final=1' and adding
'-fvisibility-inlines-hidden' to th g++ command-line, link time drops
to a few seconds !

I'll still have to find, wether 'final=1' or
'-fvisibility-inlines-hidden' is responsible (or maybe the debug
symbols are a problem), but I was quite astonished to see such a
tremendous speed increase.
