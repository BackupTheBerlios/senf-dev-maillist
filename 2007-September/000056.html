Hi all,

I have good news: The newest incarnation of 'fix-links.py' is able to
fix *all* important links. I deem links to be important if

a) they are not whithin one of the 'source code' HTML files (the files
   which contain the highlighted source code)
b) they are not links to some line number within one of the above
   files

Fixing means finding the correct target not removing the link. All
links not fixable this way are removed but that's not too bad.

The bad news is, that doxygen really produces some funky link errors
... no idea what it's thinking there.
