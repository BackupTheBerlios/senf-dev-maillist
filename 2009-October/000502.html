Thorsten Horstmann <Thorsten.Horstmann at fokus.fraunhofer.de> writes:
> I'm already using your new feature. Great work! Thanks!
> One question: Why do I need to change in the subdirectory? Why
> scons senf/Packets/80211Bundle/test sparse_tests=1 
> only_tests=WLANBeaconPacket.test.cc
> doesn't work.

Because you then need to add the path:

  # scons senf/Packets/80211Bundle/test only_tests=senf/Packets/80211Bundle/WLANBeaconPacket.test.cc

Three additional points: a) When giving only_tests, you don't need
sparse_tests. b) You can also give more then a single test in
'only_tests', even from different test suites. And c) Since only the
tests enabled are defined you can just use 'all_tests' as target:

  # scons all_tests only_tests=senf/Packets/80211Bundle/WLANBeaconPacket.test.cc

or

  # scons all_tests only_tests="senf/Packets/80211Bundle/WLANBeaconPacket.test.cc senf/Utils/type_traits.test.cc"

which is a little bit weird but works :-)
