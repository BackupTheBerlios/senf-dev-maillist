Hi,

after looking at the new IPv6Packet-Extensions in SENF I want to remind 
  packet/parser implementors to the naming conventions for parser 
classes: parser classes ends with "Parser"(!), parser classes with a 
corresponding PacketType class (in the documentation they are sometimes 
called protocol parsers) ends with "PacketParser". For example we have 
an "IPv4PacketParser" which is the parser for IPv4PacketType. Parser 
classes without a PacketType have no "PacketParser" in their name; for 
example MACAddressParser or Int32Parser.

Therefore the class "IPv6PacketParserExtension_Routing" for example 
should be renamed to "IPv6RoutingExtensionParser", right?

Comments, objections?

  -Thorsten
