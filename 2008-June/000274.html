up to now the protocols folder containing all the link layer address, 
sockaddress, tapdevice and packetsocket classes is 
calles "Socket/Protocols/Raw". 
This has nothing to do with the content but sounds like INet-Rawsockets (which 
belong to the INet-Family and folder).

Sockets/Protocols/Link
Sockets/Protocols/L2
Sockets/Protocols/MAC

change the include lines in the projects depending on these objects (maybe 
just 2, definitely not so many yet). 

At the moment I think the minimalistic L2 fits best, but...
