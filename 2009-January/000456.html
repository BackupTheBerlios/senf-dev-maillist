Hi everyone,

after getting a bugreport by someone trying to compile SENF on
intrepid against boost-1.35, I have rechecekd, that SENF works. After
adding some fixes, SENF now compiles on intrepid against boost-1.37
(and 1.36 and I excpect against 1.35 also). So SENF now works using

 - g++ 4.3
 - glibc 2.8
 - boost 1.37

which are all quite recent. After some minor patching of boost-1.37
(the patches are already in boost-trunk), the compile even went
through with -Werror.

I did NOT check the documentation compile, just the all_tests target.

stefan.
